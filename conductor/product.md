# Initial Concept
The user wants to build a clinic management application using Next.js, Clerk, Prisma, Supabase, TanStack Query, Zustand, Tailwind CSS 4, and shadcn/ui.

# Product Definition

## Target Users
- **Clinic Administrators:** Managing staff, billing, scheduling, and RBAC.
- **Doctors & Medical Staff:** Managing patient encounters, digital prescriptions, and lab orders.
- **Patients:** Booking appointments, viewing medical history, and managing profiles via a patient portal.
- **Pharmacy & Lab Staff:** Managing inventory, dispensing medications, and processing lab orders.

## Core Goals
- **Streamline Operations:** Efficiently manage scheduling, check-ins, and billing processes.
- **Enhance Patient Care:** Digitally manage health records, prescriptions, and clinical encounters.
- **Patient Empowerment:** Provide a secure portal for easy booking and access to medical records.
- **Inventory Optimization:** Accurately track pharmacy inventory and streamline dispensing.

## MVP Features
- **Role-Based Dashboards:** Specialized views for Admins, Doctors, Pharmacy, and Staff.
- **Patient Management:** Registration, check-in, and appointment booking systems.
- **Clinical Workflows:** Encounter forms, digital prescriptions, and lab order management.
- **Pharmacy Operations:** Inventory tracking and medication dispensing.
- **Financial Module:** Billing and payment processing for clinic services.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Security & Compliance:** Alignment with HIPAA/GDPR standards for sensitive medical data.
- **High Availability:** Ensure 24/7 access to critical clinic operations.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile use by medical staff and patients.
- **Performance:** Fast, low-latency interfaces for clinical data entry and retrieval.

## Aesthetic & Visual Guidelines
- **Professional & Clean:** Prioritize readability and clarity for high-stakes medical environments.
- **Modern & Minimalist:** Utilize shadcn/ui and Tailwind CSS 4 for a crisp, contemporary interface.
- **Accessible:** Ensure high contrast and large interactive elements for a diverse patient demographic.
